## Power BI Dashboards

This section showcases a selection of **Power BI dashboards** developed to analyse commercial performance, financial results, and public sector data. Across all projects, I applied robust **data modelling and advanced Power BI techniques** to ensure accurate analysis and meaningful insights.

### How I approach Power BI projects

Across all dashboards, I follow a consistent approach to ensure accuracy, scalability, and clarity for non-technical users.

- Data transformation and cleaning using **Power Query**
- Star-schema data modelling with **separated fact and dimension tables**
- Management of **relationships** to ensure accurate filtering and aggregation
- Development of **advanced DAX measures** for KPIs, time intelligence, and variance analysis
- Use of **interactive slicers, drill-downs, drill-throughs, and custom tooltips**
- Design of dashboards with a focus on **clarity, usability, and business relevance**
